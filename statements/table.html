Solidarity Table

June 28, 2024

This intimate table brings the queer community and the Palestinian resistance together in its design motifs and exists for the sake of holding revolutionary conversations--conversations that center the everyday strategizing of collective resistance to oppression and the envisioning of liberated futures.

All of the legs and supporting structural beams feature the colors of the Progress Pride flag–red, yellow, orange, green, blue, purple, pink, light blue, black, brown, and white–which offer an important corrective to the six-colored rainbow flag. These colors are inclusive of trans people and people of color who have historically been marginalized even within the queer liberation movement.

The table-top features the fishnet, olive leaves, and stripes (representing Palestinians’ connection to the sea, relationship to the land, and historical trade routes, respectively) which form the three major design motifs from the keffiyeh. The keffiyeh has become an icon of the Palestinian resistance worldwide and is often worn by those wishing to express solidarity with their struggle.

Arranged so, the table symbolizes solidarity and commitment to shared struggle by visually representing the queer community uplifting the Palestinian resistance.
