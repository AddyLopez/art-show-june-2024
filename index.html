About the Artist

Addy López is a drummer, caregiver, organizer, and software developer with deep roots in New Mexico. Queer liberation is a matter of deep personal significance to them, as a non-binary lesbian, as is the importance of intersectional collective struggle. Although their creative practice chiefly revolves around music, they enjoy working on projects that buck conventional definitions of art and that are marked by qualities of originality, inventiveness, and insight drawn from a creative process infused with experimentation and unabating contemplation.
